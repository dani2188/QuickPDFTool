Why compress PDF for email?

Many email services limit attachment sizes. Large PDF documents may not be accepted or may take a long time to upload. Compressing your PDF reduces file size while keeping the document readable.

How to compress a PDF for email

  • 1 Open the Compress PDF tool
  • 2 Upload your PDF file
  • 3 Click "Compress PDF"
  • 4 Download the smaller file
  • 5 Attach the compressed file to your email

Email attachment size limits

Different email providers allow different attachment sizes:

  • Gmail: 25 MB
  • Outlook: 20 MB
  • Yahoo Mail: 25 MB

If your PDF exceeds these limits, compressing it can help reduce its size enough to send it successfully.
