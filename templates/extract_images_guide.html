How to Extract Images from PDF

Follow these simple steps to extract images from your PDF file.

Step-by-step guide

  • 1 Open the Extract Images tool
  • 2 Upload your PDF file

Why extract images from PDF?

PDF documents often contain photos, graphics or illustrations. Extracting images allows you to reuse them in presentations, reports or websites.
